# UIG Property Acquisition — Brand & Go-To-Market Strategy

## REVISED BUSINESS MODEL

### The Companies
| Company | Role |
|---------|------|
| **UIG LLC** (uigllc.org) | US-based property buyer expanding to India. Needs land for business operations, staff quarters, corporate housing |
| **ICP3A** | International Commercial Property Accreditation & Acquisition Agency. Issues LPI (LiDAR Property Identifier) certificates |

### The Frontend Narrative
> "UIG LLC is a US-based investment and development company planning to establish operations in India. We are acquiring properties near major airports for:
> - Corporate office space
> - Staff residential quarters  
> - Logistics and distribution centers
> - Future development projects

> As part of our due diligence, we partner with ICP3A to verify property credentials through LPI certification — the international standard for commercial property verification."

### How It Works
```
Property Owner near Airport
         ↓
Wants to sell/rent property
         ↓
Discovers ICP3A LPI certification (via UIG partnership)
         ↓
Obtains LPI certificate (adds property value + credibility)
         ↓
UIG (as buyer) reviews LPI-certified properties
         ↓
Direct negotiation: UIG → Property Owner
```
